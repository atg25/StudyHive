# **Executive Summary**Provide summary information about the project and why it is needed:

Our group will create an e-commerce website to sell a dynamic, collaborative, and easily accessible learning ecosystem by combining the strengths of platforms we are all familiar with like Notion, Chegg, Quizlet, Reddit, and AI into one integrated experience. This platform aims to make it easier for students to study and share knowledge by transforming messy class notes into more structured learning resources to look back upon. Our platform will have AI summaries, personalized study packets, interactive flashcards, and Khan Academyâ€“style video explanations all of which will be communal for users to share and learn from. We envision a future where every student, no matter the grade level, will use our platform as their primary learning resource to get a head in class. Ultimately, our vision is to break down barriers to learning, help students with collaboration, and empower individuals to study in a stress free way.

# **Project Purpose** With greater specificity, state the purpose of the project.

Our project purpose is to design and sell a platform where students can upload, share, and access class notes, which our AI will transform into diverse study formats. At the basic level (free version), users can browse notes and AI generated summaries to better understand concepts. The plus tier will provide better features with customized study packets, the latest AI models, and unlimited access to expert verified material, ensuring more effective education. The premium tier provides the best support with AI personalized tutoring and video walkthroughs, making it worth the cost of the subscription. We will merge collaborative knowledge sharing with AI, making it our mission to create a scalable, learning hub that becomes the next big e-commerce hit.

## **Business Objectives**

A project should support the objectives in the strategic plan. List specific strategic plan objectives and describe how the project supports each one.

**Enhance Access to Quality Education**

* Provide affordable, accessible, and high-quality study resources (demonstrated via prototype).

**Create a Unified Learning Platform**

* Showcase a single, interactive web application made by students for students.  
* Integrate familiar features from educational and social platforms to encourage engagement.  
* Demonstrate scalability through design and showing how the system could grow beyond the classroom if developed further.

# **Project Details**

Describe the project of choice in greater detail.

Our project is all based on our product goal, which is to design a functional prototype website where students can upload, share, and access class notes, which an integrated AI model will convert into multiple study formats.

The free version allows users to browse notes and AI-generated summaries. The plus tier (simulated) demonstrates enhanced features like custom study packets and verified resources. The premium tier (simulated) shows advanced concepts such as AI tutoring and video walkthroughs. Though these tiers will not involve actual transactions, they showcase how a future system could be structured if scaled for public use.
